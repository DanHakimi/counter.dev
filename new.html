The friendly alternative

We track only very little and in a way that is highly economical regarding hosting costs. This allows offering this service for free without collection data for monetization purposes. .Most web analytics services have to charge for their services or use your data for monetization purposes. We take another route, which is to track only what is essential and economical regarding to load on the server. This allows us to offer this service for free which little hosting costs, which in the future we will try to cover with donation or very conservative advertising.
