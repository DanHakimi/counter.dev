Our Privacy Policy
What data do we collect?

Counter collects the following data

  • Your username
  • An encrypted password
  • The domains that where used to access web pages containing your personalized tracking script.

Your personalized tracking script aggregates the following statistical information of your users.

  • The day of the week (as in: Monday, Tuesday, ...)
  • Wether the users device is a Phone, Tabled or Computer
  • The date of the visit
  • From which other website the visit originated.
  • The users browser (as in: Firefox, Chrome, Microsoft Edge)
  • The hour compontent of the current time in a day
  • A country mapped to the users internet access
  • The users operating system
  • Information on the users screen size
  • The users preferred language

These data points are not saved separately but anonymously aggregated on a daily, weekly, monthly, yearly and overall basis. Counter deletes daily, weekly, monthly statistics after a certain time period, further improving data privacy.

How do we collect your data?
You directly provide Counter with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you
  • Register online.
  • Write us an email.
  • Include your personalized tracking script in a web page which is visited by other users.
